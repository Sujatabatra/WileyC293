Features of OOP
1. Abstraction : Hiding Unessential details from the user of the System.
2. Data Hiding : Protecting your Data From the Outside world.
3. Encapsulation : Collecting Data and the methods those operate on that data into a single Unit.
4. Inheritance : Creation of new things with the help of existing things , without hampering the originality of existing things.
Advantage : reusability, extensibility
5. Polymorphism : One Name but multiple forms and depending upon the situation it is changing its functionality.
